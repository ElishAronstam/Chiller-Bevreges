About the founder

Chiller Beverages was founded at 2002 by Neill Levitt in Johansburg, South Africa.

Neill saw the need for a company that will sell powders and syrups that are kosher and can be distributed to different countries.

Later, his brothers -Seth and Marlon joined the company and they now run it in 3 different countries-Israel, USA and South Africa.

About Chiller

Launched in 2002, Chiller is a family run business.

It is the personal touch that has guided the success of our company and it’s our pledge to continue that passion and unique connection throughout our endeavours.

Today, these irresistible drinks could not be more popular and Chiller is leading the way with innovative flavour discoveries and an uncompromising passion for quality – being a leading international gourmet beverage supplier.

... Chiller is a proud supplier of leading franchise brands. The products we manufacture range from blended ice coffees, fruit smoothies, hot chocolates, cocktail & coffee syrups, slush, gourmet sauces, coffee beans and milkshake syrups.
